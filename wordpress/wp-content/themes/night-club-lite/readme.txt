== Description ==
Night Club Lite is a fantastic and versatile music streaming WordPress theme for the nightclubs and music industry. It is specially developed for the music bands, orchestra, radio stations, festival event, artists, restaurants and bars, DJ websites and entertainment sites. With its extremely clean and futuristic design, this theme is mainly focused on the underground music and nightclub scene.
